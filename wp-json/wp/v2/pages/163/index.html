\n\t\t\t\t\tOvicore Software<\/span>\n\t\t\t\t\t<\/h2>
<\/div><\/div>\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t\t
\n
\n
\n

Ovicore Labs is a software development company that focuses on developing and implementing augmented reality (AR) technology in universities. By bringing AR technology to educational institutions, Ovicore Labs aims to close the gap between the latest technology and traditional universities, providing students and faculty with access to modern tools and resources. This can be especially beneficial in fields such as science, technology, engineering, and math, where visual and interactive learning can be more effective than traditional methods. Ovicore Labs works with universities to design and implement AR projects that meet their specific needs and goals, helping to bring the power of modern tech to the classroom and beyond.<\/p>\n<\/div>\n<\/div>\n<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t<\/div><\/div>\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t

\n\t\t\t\t\tOvicore Services<\/span>\n\t\t\t\t\t<\/h2>
<\/div><\/div>\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t\t
\n
\n
\n

Ovicore offers a range of services that are well-suited to the needs and interests of universities, including a web-based service that allows users to request technical support and assistance, the provision of advanced AR headsets called HoloLens, and constant updates to our services and technology.
