How It Work?<\/span><\/h2>\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t\t

Augmented reality (AR) is a technology that superimposes digital information and visualizations on top of the physical world. This is achieved through the use of devices such as smartphones, tablets, or specialized AR glasses, which have sensors, displays, and software that enable them to recognize and track the user's environment.<\/p>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t<\/div><\/div>\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t<\/section>\n\t\t\t\t
